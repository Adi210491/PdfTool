    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is this PDF to Word converter free to use?

    Yes, our PDF to Word converter is completely free to use. You can convert as many files as you need without any limitations or hidden charges.

    How do I convert PDF to Word?

    Converting PDF to Word is simple with our tool: 1) Upload your PDF file, 2) Click the "Convert" button, 3) Download your Word document. The whole process takes just a few seconds.

    Is there a file size limit?

    You can convert PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend compressing them first or splitting them into smaller parts.

    Do you keep my files after conversion?

    No, we automatically delete your files from our servers after the conversion is complete. Your documents remain private and secure.

    Can I convert scanned PDFs to Word?

    Yes, you can convert scanned PDFs by enabling the OCR (Optical Character Recognition) option. This will extract text from scanned images.
